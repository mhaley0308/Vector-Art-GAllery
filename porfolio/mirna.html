My Milo Love

February 7, 2020

The name of my pet is "Milo". I named her Milo because I like to drink Milo. She is kind, sweet, noisy, and beautiful with a heart. She has a brown fur with a grey spot on her ears, she has a sister and they love to play with another. Whenever I went home, she always came to me with a smile and cling on to my legs, I love her so much.
